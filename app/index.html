📖 Family Finance — How it works

👤 Andrew · Lyn · Combined

The three buttons at the top of the screen switch whose data you're looking at. Everything — balances, transactions, spending, budgets — updates to match.

Andrew
Your accounts + joint
Lyn
Lyn's accounts + joint
Combined
All accounts

In Combined view, internal transfers between your accounts are automatically hidden so they don't inflate your spending totals.

🏠 Dashboard

Your home screen. Shows a snapshot of:

  • Total balance — sum of all visible accounts
  • Month-to-date spending — how much has gone out this month
  • Account balances — one card per account
  • Inbox count — transactions waiting for your attention

📬 Inbox

Every time transactions are imported, any that couldn't be automatically categorised land here. The badge on the tab tells you how many are waiting.

⚠️ Uncategorised — tap to assign a category

Tap any inbox item to pick a category. Once categorised, it disappears from the inbox. You can also bulk re-categorise: tap a merchant name to apply the same category to all transactions from that merchant at once.

💳 Transactions

The full list of every transaction. You can:

  • Search by description, merchant, or amount
  • Filter by account, category, or date range
  • Tap a category chip to change it on the spot
  • Flag a transaction as a business expense (B) or FL3XX expense (F) — see below
  • Attach a receipt from Google Drive (📎) — for flagged transactions

🏷️ Flags — B and F

Two special flags can be applied to any transaction:

  • B (Business) — this expense is a claimable business cost. Flagged transactions appear in the quarterly Business Expenses Report.
  • F (FL3XX) — this expense is related to a FL3XX flight or operation. Flagged transactions appear in the monthly FL3XX Report.

Tap B or F on any transaction to toggle the flag on or off. A receipt icon (📎) appears once a flag is set — tap it to attach a receipt from Google Drive.

📊 Spending

A breakdown of spending by category for the selected month. Shows how much went to groceries, dining, utilities, and so on — and how that compares to the previous month.

Tap any category bar to drill down into its transactions.

🎯 Budgets

Set a monthly spending limit for any category. A progress bar shows how much of the budget you've used. When you're over budget, the bar turns red.

Budgets can be set separately for Andrew and Lyn, or viewed as a combined household total.

🏦 Balances

A timeline of account balances over time. Useful for spotting trends — is your savings account growing? Has a credit card balance crept up?

📄 Reports

Generate PDF reports directly from the app:

  • Business Expenses Report — all transactions flagged B for a given quarter. Download as a PDF for your accountant.
  • FL3XX Report — all transactions flagged F for a given month. A Python script can also merge the actual receipt PDFs into one combined document.

☁️ Google Drive

The Drive button in the top bar connects the app to your Google account. Once connected, you can attach receipt files (photos, PDFs) directly to flagged transactions. Receipts are stored in your Drive and linked to the transaction.

You'll need to tap Drive once per session to authenticate. If the button shows ✓ Drive, you're connected.

⬆️ Monthly Import

Once a month, run the import from your laptop to pull in new transactions from BOV eBanking. The import script:

  • Opens BOV eBanking in the browser
  • Downloads CSVs for all accounts (Andrew's and Lyn's)
  • Pushes transactions to the database, skipping duplicates
  • Auto-categorises based on merchant name rules
  • Flags anything it can't categorise for your review in the Inbox

You don't need to touch a spreadsheet — just run the script and then open the app on your phone to review.
